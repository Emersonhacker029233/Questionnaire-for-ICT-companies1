Capacity Mapping Survey: Performance and Preparedness of 100% Tanzanian-Owned Suppliers and Service Providers in Reserved Mining Categories under Regulation 13A of the Mining (Local Content) Regulations, 2018 (as amended)

Purpose Statement: This survey, conducted by the Mines Suppliers and Services Providers Association of Tanzania (MSSPAT), assesses the performance and preparedness of 100% Tanzanian-owned suppliers and service providers in the mining sector. It evaluates their capacity and capabilities to deliver goods and services exclusively reserved under Regulation 13A of the Mining (Local Content) Regulations (as amended in 2025 via GN 563/2025).

Confidentiality & Consent: Responses are anonymous/confidential and will be used only for research/advocacy purposes. Participation is voluntary.

Estimated Time & Contact: Takes about 15–20 minutes. For questions: contact MSSPAT.
